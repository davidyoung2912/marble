Riptide

You against three currents. No partnerships — just your cards, your nerve, and the tide.

🎯
Bid your tricks
Once your hand is dealt, predict how many of the 13 tricks you'll win. Closest to your bid without going over earns a +3 point bonus at hand's end.
♠
Follow suit
Highest card of the suit led wins the trick — unless the tide turns.
🌊
The tide turns
Every trick has a secret rank, shuffled fresh each hand and revealed only when the trick begins. Any card of that rank wins outright, no matter what was led.
↺
Washout
A second tide card played in the same trick cancels the first — the tide washes out and the trick reverts to normal rules.
★
Scoring
1 point for a normal trick, 2 points if you win it with the tide card. First to 21 wins.
➜
Leading the next hand
Whoever wins the most tricks in a hand leads the next one — an edge worth playing for.
